Iaroslav Ponomarenko

I am a second-year master's student at Peking University's Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies, where I am fortunate to work with Professor Hao Dong at the joint Hyperplane/AGIBot PKU Lab on embodied AI, robotics, large foundation models, and computer vision. Research

My research focuses on the intersection of embodied AI, visual perception of the world, reasoning, and robotic control. Specifically, I investigate how to enable embodied agents to obtain environmental awareness through vision, including affordance understanding [P1, P2, p1, p3] and spatial reasoning [p2], in order to perform complex manipulation tasks. Currently, I am investigating these areas using large multimodal foundation models.
